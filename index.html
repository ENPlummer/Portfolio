About Me

Ebony is data analyst with a deep curiosity, and an untiring desire to find the truth. She believes that data should do the talking. She views that technology should be a tool to improved organizations and communities. Here defining moment that lead her to want to enter the technology was seeing the Google doodle of Ada Lovelace. By learning about Ada’s Lovelace’s contribution to the field of computer science help Ebony realized her passion for technology and data. Ebony specializes in data analytics and visualizations. The highlights of Ebony’s work include WeatherPy, an analysis of the correlation of latitude to various types of weather, an analysis of the sentiment expressed in the tweets of major news organizations, and created an API that allows uses to search for past weather data. In addition to her technical skills she also has strong organizational skills in December 2016 she was awarded “Outstanding Team” Award for being part of the who revamped her organization’s administrative procedures. Ebony holds a certificate in Data Science and Visualization from George Washington University. She also has a degree in Foreign Affairs from the University of Virginia.
